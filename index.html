Covid-19 sucks!

Teens and young adults have been hit harder by Covid-19 than any other age group.

Taking care of yourself during Covid

A pandemic is a stressful time for everyone. If you've been suffering with bouts of anxiety or depression, even if you never have before, you aren't alone. The CDC and WHO recommend maintaining healthy daily habits and routines to keep yourself on track during times of confusion.

According to recent data, even people who have never had mental health issues have become unstable during this pandemic.
