CDC Data

Teens and young adults have been hit harder by Covid-19 than any other age group.



According to CDC Pulse statistics, young adults (ages 18-29) have suffered the most from symptoms of anxiety and depression during the Covid-19 pandemic. (source)




Hawaii is ranked second worst in the nation in terms of symptoms of anxiety and depression during Covid-19. (source)


About the data

Every five days, the percentage of young adults (ages 18-29) experiencing symptoms of anxiety has trended upward. If we work together to help out this age group, we can stop this upward trend.
